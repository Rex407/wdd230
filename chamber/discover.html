Discover Lilongwe

Demographics

Population: ~1.2 million (2025 estimate)

Median Age: 20 years

Urban Growth Rate: 4.3% annually

Languages: English (official), Chichewa (widely spoken)

Key Statistics

Economic Hub: Lilongwe contributes ~20% to Malawi's GDP

Businesses: Over 5,000 registered businesses

Tourism: ~150,000 visitors annually

Attractions

Lilongwe Wildlife Centre: A sanctuary for rescued animals.

Old Town Market: Vibrant market with local crafts.

Kamuzu Mausoleum: Historical landmark honoring Malawi's first president.
