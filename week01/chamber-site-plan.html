Site Plan

Site Name

The name of our site is Lilongwe Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the city of Lilongwe in Malawi and its commitment to supporting and promoting local businesses. The word "Chamber" signifies the purpose of uniting various business entities into a supportive community.

Site Purpose

The Lilongwe Chamber of Commerce aims to:

  • Support local businesses by providing networking opportunities and resources to enhance economic growth.
  • Promote community events, business directories, and economic initiatives to strengthen local commerce.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • How can I get involved with local community initiatives?

Color Schema

The site's color scheme includes:

  • Primary Color: #0047AB (Deep Blue) - Used for headers, navigation, and call-to-action buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Gold) - Used for accents, highlights, and important details.

Typography

We will use the following fonts:

  • Lora - for headings (Elegant and professional)
  • Open Sans - for body text (Clean and readable)
